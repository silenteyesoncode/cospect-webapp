How do we use your data?

Our team has developed technology to identify symptoms of cough sound, such as dry cough and shortness of breath. Once you click submit, your cough sound will be sent to our backend server, where we will analyze the symptoms using our open source software CoSpect.

We also store your cough sound in our anonymized dataset in our backend. Being wary of privacy concerns, we do not store any personal information associated with the submission, such as your IP address, other than what you fill in yourself.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at info@konect-co.com.
